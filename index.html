How to Cook Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 4 large tomatoes (or 1 cup tomato paste)
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger (grated)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 cup cooked chicken or beef (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Blend the tomatoes, red bell peppers, and half of the onion into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and saute the chop onion, ginger, and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the blended tomato mixture and cook for about 10 minutes until it thickens.
  4. Add thyme, curry powder, paprika, black pepper, and salt. Stir well
  5. Pour in the broth and bring to boil.
  6. Rinse the rice and add it to the pot. Stir to combine.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is tender.
  8. Add cooked chicken or beef, and mixed well.
  9. Serve hot with fried plantains or salad. Enjoy!
